Our Philosophy

Fewer pieces,
better worn.

Vela was founded by designer Elara Vance after years of witnessing industry waste. We believe in creating a concise collection where every garment earns its place in your wardrobe.

Our fabrics are sourced from suppliers who share our commitment to ethical production. Each style is tested and refined for fit, feel, and durability before it reaches you.
