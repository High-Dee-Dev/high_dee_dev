Frequently Asked Questions

Common questions about our pricing and engagement models

The monthly fee includes all consultant time, strategy sessions, implementation support, progress reviews, and access to our tools and resources. There are no hidden fees or additional charges for standard services. Any custom software licenses or third-party tools would be billed separately with your approval.

Yes, you can upgrade or downgrade your plan at any time. If you upgrade, the new rate applies from your next billing cycle. If you downgrade, the change takes effect at the end of your current billing period, and any unused services from your current plan will be prorated.

Yes, we offer a 15% discount for annual commitments on Professional and Enterprise plans. This discount applies when you pay for 12 months upfront. We also offer special pricing for non-profit organizations and educational institutions.

Additional consultant hours are available at a discounted rate for existing clients. For Professional plan clients, additional hours are billed at $150/hour. For Enterprise clients, we can adjust your plan to accommodate increased needs. We'll always discuss and agree on any additional work before it begins.

For the Starter plan, there's no minimum commitment – it's project-based. For Professional and Enterprise monthly plans, we require a 3-month minimum commitment to ensure we can deliver meaningful results. After the initial 3 months, you can cancel with 30 days' notice.

We offer a 100% satisfaction guarantee for the first 30 days of any engagement. If you're not completely satisfied with our services during this period, we'll refund your payment. For project-based work, we also offer milestone-based payments to align with your comfort level and our delivery.
