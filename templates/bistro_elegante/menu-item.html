Pan-Seared Salmon

$28
Gluten Free Healthy 580 Cal

Our Atlantic salmon is pan-seared until perfectly tender, then served with fresh seasonal vegetables and finished with our house-made lemon butter sauce. It’s a guest favorite for good reason: simple, balanced, and full of flavor.

Ingredients

  • Fresh Atlantic salmon fillet
  • Seasonal vegetables, including asparagus and cherry tomatoes
  • House-made lemon butter sauce
  • Fresh herbs like dill and parsley
  • Extra virgin olive oil

Allergens & Dietary
