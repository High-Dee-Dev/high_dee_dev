About Me

I build things that
actually work.

Hi — I'm High Dee Dev, a Full-Stack Web Developer with over 4 years of experience designing and shipping web applications that solve real problems for real users.

My journey started with curiosity about how websites worked under the hood. That curiosity grew into a career building everything from data-driven dashboards to multi-user platforms with complex backend logic.

I specialise in the full product lifecycle — from database schema design and RESTful API architecture to responsive, accessible front-end interfaces. My go-to stack is Laravel + MySQL on the backend and vanilla JS + Bootstrap on the frontend, but I'm always learning new tools when the job demands it.
