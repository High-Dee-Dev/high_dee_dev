How I work

A structured approach to development that ensures quality and reduces surprises

01

Understanding

I start by asking questions. What problem are we solving? Who are the users? What constraints exist? This phase is about listening, not coding.

02

Planning

I sketch architecture, plan database schema, and outline the tech stack. Good planning prevents wasted effort and ensures we're building the right thing.

03

Building

Clean, modular code with clear documentation. I build in stages, testing as I go. Version control is non-negotiable at this stage.

04

Iterating

No product is perfect from day one. I gather feedback, measure what's working, and improve based on real usage and data.
